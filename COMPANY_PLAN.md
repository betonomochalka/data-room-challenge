# LeadBox AI Company Plan

## Positioning

LeadBox AI turns the existing secure data-room app into a focused B2B SaaS product for small businesses that close deals over chat and email but need a trusted place for files, notes, and follow-ups.

The product is not a generic messenger. It is a sales-room layer: every lead gets one secure workspace where the business can share documents, track next steps, and keep client context organized.

## Core offer

- Product: secure client sales rooms with file previews, signed links, and AI-assisted follow-up briefs.
- Buyer: clinics, salons, real estate brokers, agencies, consultants, and other service businesses with repeatable client onboarding.
- Price: $99 per month per workspace, with an annual $990 prepaid plan.
- First promise: "Close more clients by putting every document, note, and next step in one client-ready room."

## $1.2M valuation model

| Metric | Target |
| --- | ---: |
| Monthly price | $99 |
| Paying workspaces | 100 |
| Monthly recurring revenue | $9,900 |
| Annual recurring revenue | $118,800 |
| SaaS revenue multiple | 10x ARR |
| Implied valuation | $1,188,000 |

The valuation case depends on proving retention, repeatable acquisition, and low-touch onboarding. The initial goal is not broad feature coverage; it is a narrow workflow that 100 businesses will pay for.

## First execution loop

1. Ship the public `/company` page as the offer.
2. Create three demo sales rooms: clinic onboarding, real estate buyer pack, agency proposal room.
3. Send direct outreach to 100 local businesses with a concrete demo link.
4. Sell 20 pilot customers at $49-$99 per month.
5. Convert repeated onboarding steps into templates and AI follow-up prompts.
6. Move retained customers to annual $990 contracts.

## Product roadmap

### Now

- Public landing page and valuation story.
- Secure data rooms and file previews from the existing app.
- Manual onboarding for first customers.

### Next

- Sales-room templates by vertical.
- Activity timeline per client room.
- AI summary of unread files and missing next steps.
- Stripe billing for monthly and annual plans.

### Later

- Chat ingestion from WhatsApp Business, Telegram, and email.
- Team permissions and audit trail.
